About Charlton Tech Guy

Hi, I'm Eldon Elledge. I live in Charlton, MA, and I've spent decades working in software engineering, systems architecture, AI, and business technology leadership.

This site exists for a simpler reason: people and small businesses often need someone local who can explain the problem, fix what can be fixed, and help them make better technology decisions without making the process stressful.

My approach is simple: I don't expect you to know technical terms, and I won't make you feel like you should. If I use a word you don't understand, that's my mistake, not yours. I explain things clearly because technology should be understandable — not something you need a degree to deal with.

I also run Peak Agile Solutions, a consulting business focused on AI adoption, software delivery, modernization, and technology leadership. Charlton Tech Guy is the local, practical side of that experience — focused on helping individuals and small businesses with everyday technology problems without the corporate approach.
